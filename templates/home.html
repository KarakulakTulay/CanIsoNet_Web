CanIsoNet: Cancer-specific Isoform Interaction Network

CanIsoNet serve as a gateway to understand the functional role of cancer-specific isoform switching event. We provide two main functionalities to the users: Browsing various statistics on cancer-specific isoform switching events in each 1209 cancer samples from the Pan-Cancer Analysis of Whole Genomes (PCAWG) project Exploring and visualising isoform-specific network disruptions in the STRING interaction network.
